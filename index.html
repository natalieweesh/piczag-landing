Don't just capture the moment - create it.

PicZag is an iPhone App that lets friends take and share photos in a fun unique way. Every photo becomes the backdrop for friends to create their own masterpieces. Whether it's a cute collage or funny stickers, friends have never been able to share photos like this before!

With PicZag, your pictures become canvases for collaborative editing. Snap a shot, send to friends, and let the creativity flow.

Enhance your pictures

Make every minute one that counts with stickers, collages, and your own unique edits. Work, school, and the bus have never looked this good!

Edit together

Don't let your friends get the last punch. Use all of our editing features to turn the picture you received into the picture you want! Any picture becomes your canvas.

Bring more friends in the fun

On PicZag, you can collaborate with as many friends as you want. Create groups for easy sharing with one, two, three or more! See how your friends see things differently.

Share it with the world

The world is your stage! Share your individual or collaborate pictures to the PicZag Hall of Fame or to your other social media outlets, and let everyone experience the fun.

Explore
